Rome, Italy

About Italy:

Rome, the capital of Italy, was thought to be founded in 753 BC by Romulus, making it older than Italy itself. Cats are very common in Rome as they are free to move around and have special rights set by the law since 1991, to protect them. It is known as the "Eternal city", offering a vibrant experience with art, cuisine and rich culture.

My experience:

Experiencing Rome is like walking through a grand open-air museum, where every corner holds a piece of history. From the awe-inspiring Colosseum to the timeless beauty of the Trevi Fountain, the city's landmarks tell stories of ancient civilizations. Wandering through the cobbled streets, you're surrounded by stunning architecture and vibrant culture. Whether enjoying a gelato or traditional Italian pizza, Rome captivates the senses and leaves a lasting impression on all who visit.
